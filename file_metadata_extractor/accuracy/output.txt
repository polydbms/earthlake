short_description
A universal remote sensing foundation model using a novel 3D generative pretrained transformer (GPT) purpose-built for spectral RS images. Accommodates varying sizes, resolutions, time series, and regions with 3D token generation for spatial-spectral coupling and multi-target reconstruction. Trained on one million spectral RS images with over 600 million parameters.
SpectralGPT is the first universal remote sensing foundation model purpose-built for spectral remote sensing images, leveraging a novel 3D generative pretrained transformer (GPT) architecture. It is trained on over one million Sentinel-2 spectral images and supports flexible input sizes, resolutions, time series, and regions, with models up to 600 million parameters.
